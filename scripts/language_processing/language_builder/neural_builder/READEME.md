The neural builder is built on the top of `TN-PCFG` (https://github.com/sustcsonglin/TN-PCFG)
For more details of the TN-PCFG, please refer to https://github.com/sustcsonglin/TN-PCFG, and
their work:
- NAACL2021 PCFGs Can Do Better: Inducing Probabilistic Context-Free Grammars with Many Symbols
- ACL2021 Neural Bi-Lexicalzied PCFG Induction.
- NAACL2022 Dynamic Programming in Rank Space: Scaling Structured Inference with Low-Rank HMMs and PCFGs .
- EMNLP2023 Findings Simple Hardware-Efficient PCFGs with Independent Left and Right Productions
